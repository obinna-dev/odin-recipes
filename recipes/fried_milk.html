Fried Milk

Description

We know, it sounds weird... but this is a sweet dessert with a delicate flavor of nutmeg. It's crispy on the outside, smooth and custardy on the inside and guaranteed to F'up your lactose intolerance- you might die.
Highly recommend!

Ingredients

  • 1⁄2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1⁄2 cup cornstarch
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter or 1 tablespoon margarine
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon grated lemon rind
  • 2 eggs, well beaten (your tummy will feel this way after the meal)
  • 3⁄4 cup progresso plain breadcrumbs
  • 1⁄3 cup powdered sugar (optional)
  • vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Mix granulated sugar, cornstarch and nutmeg in 3-quart saucepan.
  2. Gradually stir in milk.
  3. Heat to boiling over medium heat, stirring constantly.
  4. Boil and stir 1 minute; remove from heat.
  5. Stir in butter and lemon peel.
  6. Spread evenly in ungreased square baking dish, 8x8x2 inches.
  7. Refrigerate uncovered at least 3 hours until firm.
  8. Cut custard into 2-inch squares, using wet knife.
  9. Dip custard squares into eggs, then coat with bread crumbs.
  10. Heat oil (1 to 1/2 inches) to 360°F; fry 2 or 3 squares at a time in oil 1 to 2 minutes or until light brown; drain on paper towels.
  11. Sprinkle with powdered sugar.
