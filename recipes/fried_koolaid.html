Fried Kool-Aid

Description

“The newest in fried foods. For very sweet treats use sweetened Kool-aid use (up to 1/2 c); for less sweet use the unsweetened (1/4 c)”
Guaranteed to shorten your lifespan. Highly recommend!

Ingredients

  • 1⁄4 cup sweetened Kool-Aid drink mix
  • 3 eggs
  • 1⁄4 sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 cups milk
  • 4 cups of flour

Steps

  1. Make the batter by beating the eggs and sugar together.
  2. Gradually add the milk and dry ingredients.
  3. Preheat oil to 350°F.
  4. Cook in hot vegetable oil until golden brown (a few minutes, depending on heat).
  5. Cool on paper-towel covered tray for a few minutes, then dig in!
